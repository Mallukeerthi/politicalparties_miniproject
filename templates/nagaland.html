State Emblem

The emblem of Nagaland features the state's name in English, along with traditional tribal motifs. The design symbolizes the state's rich cultural heritage and its commitment to progress and development.

Political Information

Ruling Party: Nationalist Democratic Progressive Party (NDPP)

The Nationalist Democratic Progressive Party is a regional political party in Nagaland. It was formed in 2017 and has been gaining significant political influence in the state. The party focuses on development and welfare of the people of Nagaland.

Opposition Party: Indian National Congress (INC)

The Indian National Congress is one of the oldest political parties in India, founded in 1885. It played a crucial role in India's independence movement and has been a major political force in post-independence India. The party's platform is generally considered to lie in the centre of Indian politics.
