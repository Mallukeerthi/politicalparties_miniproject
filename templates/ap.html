State Emblem

The emblem of Andhra Pradesh features the Poorna Kumbham (full pot) with two green leaves and a coconut on top, symbolizing prosperity. The design is surrounded by the name of the state in Telugu and English.

Political Information

Ruling Party: Telugu Desam Party (TDP)

The Telugu Desam Party is a regional political party in Andhra Pradesh. Founded in 1982 by N.T. Rama Rao, the party has been a significant force in Andhra Pradesh politics. The party's ideology is centered around Telugu pride and regional development.

Opposition Party: Indian National Congress (INC)

The Indian National Congress is one of the oldest political parties in India, founded in 1885. It played a crucial role in India's independence movement and has been a major political force in post-independence India. The party's platform is generally considered to lie in the centre of Indian politics.
