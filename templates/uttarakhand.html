State Emblem

The emblem of Uttarakhand features the state's name in Hindi script, along with traditional motifs. The design symbolizes the state's rich cultural heritage and its commitment to progress and development.

Political Information

Ruling Party: Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)

The Bharatiya Janata Party is a major political party in India. In Uttarakhand, it has been working towards the state's development and welfare of its people.

Opposition Party: Indian National Congress (INC)

The Indian National Congress is one of the major political parties in India. In Uttarakhand, it serves as the main opposition party, advocating for various state-level issues.
